### Short Bio

Frank Han is a Research Associate at the University of Calgary. He is an environmental modeler, programmer, and chemist. His research focuses on the development of open-source numerical models and scientific software. His aspiration is to develop powerful, comprehensive, and also easy-to-use hydrological and water quality modeling tools that can support research, decision-making, and environmental management.
