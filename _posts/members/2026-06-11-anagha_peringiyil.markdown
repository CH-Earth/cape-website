### Short Bio

Anagha Peringiyil is a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Calgary. Her research focuses on the development of an ensemble precipitation datasets, the application of advanced machine learning techniques such as Graph Neural Networks in hydrology, process-based hydrologic modeling, and Remote Sensing. Anagha has published her work in leading international journals, including Scientific Data and the International Journal of Climatology. Prior to her academic career, she worked as a Senior Data Analyst at Subex in Bangalore, India. Her long-term research vision is to integrate geospatial technologies, artificial intelligence, and hydrological sciences to address challenges related to climate change, water security, and disaster resilience.
