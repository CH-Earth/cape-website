### Short Bio
Neharika is a PhD Candidate at IIT Delhi, India and a Visiting PhD Researcher at the University of Calgary. Her research focuses on large-sample hydrological model calibration and regionalization over the Indian Subcontinent and assessing the impact of climate change on water cycle variables.
